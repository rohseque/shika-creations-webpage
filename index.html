We Are...

Shika Creations handcrafting terracotta jewellery and home decor products since 2015. Even though Shika Creations started its journey by making only traditional jewellery, it revived the dwindling art of Terracotta Jewellery by introducing blends with metals, pearls, tassel, pom – poms, kundan and kemp stones. Its expertise lies in making customized and unique terracotta jewellery from traditional to trendy ones with perfection and meeting the expectations of modern women. Shika Creations owns the pride of designing an exclusive bridal set, first of its kind in terracotta jewellery. Creations also include home décor products like themed fridge magnets, wind chimes, wall hangings and seasonal products like Rakhis. Products are sold in few retail shops in Coimbatore but mainly through online.
